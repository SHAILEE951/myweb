HI!

Tim Berners-Lee is a software engeineer who invented the Intenet (or the World Wide Web) in 1989 while at CERN, the European Particle Pysics Laboratory. He is director of the World Wide Wed Consortiun that he founded and a Professor in the Computer Science Department at the University of Oxford. in 2001 he became a Fellow of the Royal Society and in 2004 he was knighted.

In 1994 Berners-Lee founded the World Wide Web Consortiun (W3C) at the Laboratory of Computer Science (LCS) at the massachusetts Institute of Techonology in Boston. It was made up of companies that wanted to work together to create Web standards and also improve the Web. It was quickly agrees that the technology should be free of royalties and patents. Berners-Lee has served as director of this W3C since to was founded. in 1995 Microsoft produced that first version of the Internet Explorer and this commercial browser created enormous growth in websites and web users. The Web was first used for e-commerce in 1997.
